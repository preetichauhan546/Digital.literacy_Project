##### **Stay Safe Online Checklist** 



***1. Use Strong Passwords***

* Combine letters, numbers, and symbols.  
* Avoid birthdays or easy-to-guess words.  
* Use a password manager to store them securely.  



***2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)***

* Add extra security for email, social media, and banking apps.  
* Prefer authenticator apps over SMS for 2FA.  



**3. Beware of Phishing Messages**

* Don’t click suspicious links in emails, messages, or pop-ups.  
* Always verify the sender before sharing personal information.  



**4. Limit Sharing Personal Information**

* Avoid posting sensitive details like full address, phone number, or ID.  
* Adjust social media privacy settings to control who sees your posts.  



**5. Financial / UPI Safety**

* Never share OTPs, PINs, or banking passwords, even if someone claims to be a bank representative. 
* Double-check UPI/QR payments: only scan QR codes from trusted sources and verify recipient details before confirming.  



**6. Keep Software Updated**

* &#x20;Regularly update phone, apps, and antivirus to protect against malware and security vulnerabilities.  



**7. Be Careful With Public Wi-Fi**

* Avoid logging into sensitive accounts or making transactions on unsecured networks.  
* Use a VPN if public Wi-Fi is necessary.  



**8. Report and Block Abusive or Suspicious Accounts**

* Block and report any account that harasses, scams, or threatens you online.
